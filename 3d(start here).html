3D Modeling


3D House

Ever since my 3D modelling class, this has been one of my passions and easily one of my favourite creations. When creating this mini kitchen no detail was left off, right down to the handles on the fridge and such. Every piece of this kitchen is fleshed out and has some real world aspect to it. The program i used to create this model was Maya. I hope to grow better in it so i would be able to create more in depth designs and eventualy add colour and texture to my creations.
